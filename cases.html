From the knowledge base

Members search the full KB through the agent. A few public playbooks below.

kb/playbook/last-mile.md

打法:vibe coding 之后的最后一公里

Demo 不等于产品。缺的通常是:部署(域名/服务器/进程守护)、支付(收款通道)、身份(密钥身份免账号)、稳定性(看门狗/日志/备份)。CTO 档就是陪你走完这段。

Playbook: the last mile after vibe coding

A demo is not a product. What's usually missing: deploys (domain/server/supervision), payments, identity (key-based, no accounts), stability (watchdog/logs/backups). The CTO tier walks it with you.

kb/playbook/free-to-paid.md

打法:免费转付费的开关

明码标价。不亮价格,你的每次回答都默认免费。价格牌放在页面上,由页面替你报价,关系不伤。先给价值,再给价格链接。

Playbook: the free-to-paid switch

Put the price on the wall. Without a visible price, every answer you give defaults to free. Let the page quote for you — the relationship stays intact. Value first, then the pricing link.
